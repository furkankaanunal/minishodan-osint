MiniShodan OSINT Workbench

A detailed, browser-based passive OSINT dashboard for domain reconnaissance, evidence collection, Shodan-style infrastructure notes, technology profiling, severity classification, and report drafting.

Passive OSINT onlyDNS + subdomainsShodan/Censys workflowReport-ready outputGitHub Pages compatible

Ethical Scope

This tool is designed for educational passive reconnaissance. It does not perform port scanning, brute force, exploitation, login attempts, or unauthorized access.

Important: Shodan/Censys/BuiltWith/Wappalyzer findings should be manually reviewed and documented with screenshots. Treat results as exposure indicators, not confirmed vulnerabilities.
